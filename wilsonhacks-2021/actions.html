What Can I Do?

There are many ways you can support local businesses. Here are some suggestions:

  • Use delivery or pickup
  • Use online services/buy online
  • Buy giftcards or credit cards for later
  • Share things like menus and catalogs on social media
  • Donate to local businesses
  • Tip extra
  • Build a website or create a social media account for them
