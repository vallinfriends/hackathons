Why Support Local Businesses?

There are many benefits to supporting local businesses, both for the businesses and the customers.

Stronger Community
  • Local businesses tend to donate more to nonprofits than nonlocal owners.
  • Stronger relationships can be built between local business employees and regular customers.

Economic Growth
  • Local businesses provide the most new jobs.
  • Buying from local businesses keeps more money in the community, as many local businesses buy from other local businesses.

Better Shopping Experience
  • You get better customer service, as the employees typically have more specific expertise in their product areas.
  • You'll get more customized products/services, as customer desires and values matter more in small, local businesses.
